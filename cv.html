    • Advisor: Matthew S. Mizuhara, PhD

  • FRACTAL GEOMETRY AND MEASURE THEORY
    • Advisor: Edward K. Voskanian, PhD
    • SPRING 2020 - PRESENT
    • Guided Study
    • Studied the theory of Fractal Geometry and Complex Dimensions due to Lapidus and van Frankenhuijsen. Learned the required amount of Measure Theory to understand the concept of a Measure, an Outer-Measure, and a Pre-Measure.
